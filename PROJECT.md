**Project Name**: *Schedula* (working name)

**Product Type**: AI-Powered Timetable Optimization SaaS

**Target Users**: Universities, Colleges, Academic Institutions (Admins, Department Heads, Lecturers, Students)

---

## 🎯 Objective

To build a smart, AI-powered SaaS platform that automatically generates **conflict-free, optimized semester class routines** for students, lecturers, and departments — eliminating manual scheduling, reducing errors, and saving time across academic institutions.

---

## 🧨 Problem Statement

Most universities and colleges still create semester class routines **manually**, leading to:

- **Time-consuming** planning processes
- Frequent **classroom/lecturer conflicts**
- **Student schedule clashes** (two classes at once)
- Inefficient use of classrooms and faculty time
- Revisions that delay semester kickoffs

---

## ✅ Our Solution

**Schedula** automates the entire routine planning process using **AI-powered optimization algorithms**. The platform allows academic admins to upload basic semester data and instantly generate optimized, conflict-free schedules that:

- Respect **lecturer availability**
- Ensure **no overlapping classes** for students
- Efficiently assign **rooms based on capacity and type**
- Minimize **student idle time**
- Allow **manual overrides with real-time conflict detection**

---

## 💡 Key Features (MVP)

- CSV/form-based data upload (courses, lecturers, rooms, student batches)
- AI-based routine generation engine
- Conflict detection & resolution (student, room, faculty level)
- Weekly calendar views (per department, lecturer, batch)
- Routine export as PDF/Excel
- Simple user roles: Admin, Lecturer, Student (view-only)

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Target Users

- University & college administrators
- Department scheduling officers
- Lecturers and faculty members
- Students (to view personalized timetables)

---

## 🚀 Benefits

- Save **dozens of hours** per semester in planning
- Eliminate **human errors** in scheduling
- Enable **faster semester launches**
- Provide **transparency** and visibility for all academic stakeholders
- Create **adaptable schedules** for dynamic academic environments
